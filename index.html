Just who is Jordan Van Ness?

Washingtonian - 20something - 3d artist - musician - programmer (maybe)

Bio:

On November 21, 1992... Louise Karlsson set the world swimming record for the 50m butterfly. But on the next day, Jordan was born. Since then, Jordan has been doing things. With a passion for music, puzzles, role-playing games, and floral shirts, Jordan hopes to accomplish things that let him make a meaningful contribution to the world and help others. Jordan is from the very green and very rainy state of Washington. He has lived there for 20 years out of the 24 years he has been alive, and doesn't plan on stopping. He pretty much likes it there, I guess. Go figure. Jordan is typically found at his computer either wasting time browsing the web, listening to seemingly intentionally bad music, or making characters for role-playing games that act as a quick-dry cement for his reputation of nerdiness.

Education and Experience Timeline:

  • 2014 - Created an application using Unity 2D and JavaScript for KickShot, an organization with the goal of teaching children how to play soccer.
  • 2015 - Graduated from University of Idaho with a BS in Virtual Technology and Design, as well as a minor in Computer Science.
  • 2016 - Began working remotely as a 3D environment artist for MISC Games, developing buildings for the simulation game, Fishing: Barents Sea.
  • 2017 - Enrolled in Code Fellows, a programming boot camp, in order to drive his career down the path of a web developer.

Jordan's Goals

  • Graduating from Code Fellows and become a software developer, hopefully in the area that he wants to live
  • Continuing to find new challenges in life, and never giving up on solving puzzles and learning new things
  • Helping other people achieve their goals as well
  • Making a longer list of goals
